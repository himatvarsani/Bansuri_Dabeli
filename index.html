GIVING BACK, WHAT INSPIRED US...

When lockdown hit, many businesses were affected. There was a lot of uncertainty and even we at Bansuri Dabeli, didn‚Äôt quite know what to expect.

Watching the news and reading the papers gave us a picture of what was really happening - people losing jobs, food banks drying up and our NHS system under extreme pressure. Nobody knew how bad circumstances would get.

The situation inspired us to help the local community as much as we could. We wanted to give back, we wanted to help in any way we could. We contacted the local hospitals to offer staff on the front line fresh tasty food from day one of the lockdown.

Doctors and nurses working tirelessly and selflessly everyday and putting others first was the story up and down the country. The NHS hero‚Äôs deserved all the help they could get and keeping their energy levels up is something we wanted to help with. We prepped 250 meals everyday at the start of lockdown back in March 2020.

As the days hit and the lockdown deepened, and with the help of family & friends, we put together a system in an empty school to prep, weigh and pack food with the help of local community drivers and chefs to deliver 3000 meals per day.

We continued to do what we could until mid August when the virus threat had somewhat stabilised and things at the time, began to get back to normal.

The tireless efforts of all staff at Bansuri Dabeli as well as family & friends was incredible and we couldn‚Äôt thank everyone involved enough to make it a community project. The gratitude from all the recipients was so incredible and we‚Äôre so glad to have helped make a difference to the local community.

Pravin Vagadia
